Plate Technology

Kodak Flexcel NX

The Flexcel NX produces flat top dots using Kodak's LAM process — eliminating the ink transfer unpredictability of conventional round-top-dot plates. Consistent density from makeready to the end of a run.

Same plate technology used by premium converters in Europe and the US. We run it from Bangalore at India manufacturing cost — with DHL shipping to your region.

Dot structureFlat top — consistent ink transfer
Highlight capabilityDown to 1% stable dot hold
ScreeningAM, FM, hybrid compatible

New to Kodak Flexcel NX?

Conventional plates have round tops. Under impression they spread unpredictably — that's why density shifts through a run. Flat top dots maintain consistent geometry under impression. Same ink density. Every sheet.

Conventional — Round Top

Spreads under impression — inconsistent density run to run

Kodak NX — Flat Top

Consistent geometry — same ink density every run

Faster press approvals — less density adjustment needed
Reduced make-ready waste on every job
Consistent colour run after run, job after job
Extended plate life vs conventional systems

Our Process

The File-to-Press
5S Method

Print quality is not fixed on press. It is engineered upstream. This is the system we built — for every customer, every job, every time. No competitor has named or built a system like this.

1
Study

Before a single file is touched, we understand your press — inks, substrates, anilox, quality expectations, and realistic limits. Most suppliers skip this entirely. We built our entire process around it.

We ask about: press make/model, impression settings, anilox LPI and volume, ink system, substrates, minimum dot hold target, and any recurring problems with your current supplier.
2
Set

We set expectations clearly, in writing, before prepress begins. What is achievable on your press. What isn't. How files should be prepared. What the approval criteria will be. No surprises at makeready.

3
Standardize

Every department follows standardised checklists. File intake, prepress, plate imaging, quality check, packing. Quality is a system — not who is on shift today. This is how we make it repeatable.

4
Supply

Plates manufactured on Kodak Flexcel NX and imaged on Esko CDI — engineered to the press configuration we studied in step one. Not to a generic specification sheet. Domestic delivery or DHL worldwide.

5
Support

One stable contact stays with your job until it runs predictably on press. Same person — always. Full context of your press, your history, and your standards. No handoffs. No re-explaining.

This is the one thing every competitor fails at — and the one thing every production head wants most.

About Us

Built on a belief:
quality is engineered
upstream.

Sree Bloomy Graphic House was founded in 2015 in Bangalore by K.C. Mohan — a production specialist with deep expertise in flexographic plate manufacturing and prepress.

The founding belief: most print problems are created upstream — in the file, in the prepress decisions, in the communication between customer and supplier. That's exactly where they need to be solved.

We built our facility around Kodak Flexcel NX plate technology and Esko CDI imaging. Then we built a process around those machines — the File-to-Press 5S Method. Study, Set, Standardize, Supply, Support.

Today we serve flexo converters of all sizes, across labels, flexible packaging and corrugated — in India and internationally across Asia, the Middle East, and Africa.
